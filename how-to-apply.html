How to apply?

Applying to Maluti TVET College is a streamlined digital process designed to ensure that all prospective students can secure their place in our vocational and occupational programs. It involves a sequence of essential steps, from initial career guidance to the final submission of certified documentation. When followed correctly, this process ensures a smooth transition into your chosen field of study.

What are the stages of an application?

Every successful application to Maluti TVET College consists of three primary phases. Understanding these stages will help you prepare your documents, meet deadlines, and track your status effectively.

  • Pre-application: Career interest profiling, selecting your campus, and gathering certified identification and academic results.
  • Online Submission: Completing the digital application form via the student portal and uploading required documentation.
  • Post-application: Monitoring your SMS/Email for admission status, attending placement tests if required, and finalizing registration.

Pre-application stage

Before you begin the digital form, you must complete the mandatory Pre-Entry Placement Assessment. This tool helps align your skills and interests with the correct NATED or NC(V) programmes offered across our campuses.

Required Documentation

Ensure you have clear, digital copies of the following before starting:

  • Certified copy of your latest Grade 9, 10, 11 or 12 results.
  • Certified copy of your Identity Document (ID).
  • Proof of residence.
  • A valid email address and personal mobile number for all correspondence.
