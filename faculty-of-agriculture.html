The future of food security starts with you. We blend biological science with business management to grow a prosperous, land-based economy.

The Vision
Agriculture is no longer just "farming"—it is the intersection of technology, ecology, and global trade.

In the coming decades, the world will need to produce 70% more food to feed a growing population. South Africa stands at the center of this challenge, with our rich soil and diverse climate offering a massive opportunity for young professionals.

At Maluti TVET, our Faculty of Agriculture is designed to move students past the basics. We look at the science of the soil, the genetics of livestock, and the precision of modern machinery.

What You Will Master

Curriculum Specializations

01
Plant Production
Learn the cycle of life from seed to harvest. We cover soil science, crop protection, and irrigation techniques for both sustainable organic methods and large-scale commercial production.
02
Animal Production
Master the health and management of livestock. This stream covers animal nutrition, breeding, and ethical farming practices, preparing you for roles in dairy, poultry, and meat production.
03
Agri-Business
Farming is a business. We teach you how to manage farm finances, navigate supply chains, and market your products effectively in both local and international markets.
04
Farm Planning
Understand the architecture of the land. Learn how to plan farm layouts, maintain equipment, and manage natural resources to ensure long-term sustainability.

A Career with Purpose

When you choose Agriculture, you aren't just choosing a job; you are choosing to be the person who feeds a nation. There is a deep, unmatched pride in watching a harvest grow from your own labor and intelligence.

The High-Tech Future

Think drones, automated irrigation, and data-driven harvest cycles. Modern agriculture is one of the most technologically advanced sectors in the world. If you love science and technology, there is no better place to innovate.

Financial Independence

Agriculture is a gateway to entrepreneurship. Many of our graduates go on to start their own small-scale farms or consulting agencies, creating wealth and jobs within their communities.

Climate Leadership

Our students are on the front lines of the fight against climate change. By learning sustainable farming, you help restore the earth's health while ensuring our food systems remain resilient.
