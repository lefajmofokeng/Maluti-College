NCV PROGRAM
Program Objective

The Language of Money

Economic stability is the foundation of a thriving society. The Finance, Economics, and Accounting NCV program is designed for students who possess a sharp eye for detail and a passion for numbers. This course goes beyond simple bookkeeping; it teaches you how wealth is created, managed, and audited in the modern global economy.

Empowering Your Financial Future

Finance is one of the few industries that remains essential regardless of economic shifts. By choosing this specialized path, you aren't just learning to balance books; you are learning how to manage the lifeblood of every corporation. While others learn general theory, you will graduate with a technical mastery of financial systems that makes you an asset to any employer from day one.

Curriculum Structure (Levels 2–4)

This is a comprehensive 3-year NCV journey. Each level is an annual academic program consisting of 7 subjects designed to build progressively complex skills.

CORE

Fundamental Subjects (3)

To ensure professional communication and logical agility, students take English First Additional Language, Life Orientation, and Mathematics. (Note: Pure Mathematics is highly encouraged for the Finance stream).

TECHNICAL

Vocational Specializations (4)

The technical core includes Applied Accounting, Financial Management, Economic Environment, and New Venture Creation.

Academic Clarity: Completing Level 4 awards you a National Certificate (Vocational). This is an NQF Level 4 qualification, making it equivalent to Grade 12 (Matric). This specialization gives you a significant advantage in accuracy and professional ethics over a standard matriculant.
Financial Literacy & Ethics

Integrity in Practice

In the world of Finance, your reputation is your currency. Our program places a heavy emphasis on Professional Ethics and Financial Integrity. Students learn the legal frameworks governing South African finance, including SARS compliance and basic auditing principles, ensuring they enter the workforce as trustworthy professionals.

Tuition & Financials

Investing in Your Growth

Tuition for the Finance faculty covers academic registration, access to specialized accounting software labs, and all vocational assessment materials.

FEES

Estimated Annual Tuition

The estimated annual tuition for Finance, Economics & Accounting is R9,800 – R12,500 per year. This is a full-package fee for all 7 subjects per level.

For official quotes or NSFAS enquiry assistance, contact our Finance office at 058 713 6100.

POLICIES

Academic Progression

Because accounting principles are cumulative, students must pass their Level 2 Accounting modules before progressing to Level 3. Failed subjects must be repeated in the following academic year to satisfy the requirements for the National Certificate.

Professional Impact

Career Mobility & Growth

The NCV Finance qualification is a powerful springboard. It prepares you for the workplace while keeping the door wide open for higher academic achievement.

EMPLOYMENT

Industry Roles

Graduates are eligible for roles such as Junior Bookkeeper, Debtors/Creditors Clerk, Bank Teller, and Assistant Financial Administrator.

ADVANCEMENT

The Road to Chartered Status

Many of our top-performing students use their NCV Level 4 results to apply for Diplomas in Accounting or B-Com Degrees at Universities of Technology. Your three years of specialized focus in the TVET system means you will enter university with a deeper understanding of accounting cycles than your peers.

Academic Advice
Strong performance in Mathematics is key to success in this stream.
Ensure you have a scientific calculator and basic ledger books before the first week of lectures.
