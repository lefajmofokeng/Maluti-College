Academic Policy

What happens if you fail a subject?

Failing a module requires immediate action to ensure your progress isn't stalled indefinitely:

1. Re-Registration: For NATED, you must register for the failed subject in the very next trimester. You only pay for that specific subject.

2. The 'Chain' Rule: You cannot attempt N4 Fitting & Machining if you have not passed N3. The foundation must be solid.

3. Academic Support: Students are encouraged to attend peer-tutoring sessions at the Engineering block for subjects with high failure rates like Math and Science.
