Faculty of Humanities

Champions of
Community

Where human connection meets vocational excellence. We train the professionals who nurture, host, and educate the nation.

The Humanities faculty is dedicated to the "soft skills" that drive the hard economy—Hospitality, Tourism, and Education. We turn passion for people into professional mastery.

01
Service Excellence

Hospitality & Catering

Master the art of professional service. From culinary arts to front-of-house management, we train students to excel in the high-stakes world of international hospitality.

Hospitality Studies NCV L2 – L4 | NATED N4 – N6
02
Social Impact

Educare & Development

Shape the minds of the future. Our Educare stream focuses on early childhood development, teaching the psychological and practical foundations required for early-year educators.

Educare (ECD) NATED N4 – N6
Education & Development NCV L2 – L4
03
Global Exploration

Tourism & Travel

South Africa is a global destination. We prepare students to manage the travel experience, from tour operations to destination marketing and sustainable tourism practices.

Tourism Operations NCV L2 – L4
04
Creative Economy

Art, Design & Clothing

Where creativity meets production. This stream covers the technical aspects of clothing production and design, fostering local talent for the fashion and manufacturing industries.

Clothing Production NATED N4 – N6
